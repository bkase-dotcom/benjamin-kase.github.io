Benjamin Kase

Materials Engineer & Interaction Designer

I am a materials engineer and designer passionate about creating products where human-centered design meets material innovation. With a background in engineering, product development, and hands-on prototyping, I focus on not just inventing products, but crafting the experiences people have with them. My work explores the intersections of interaction, form, and function, blending rigorous engineering analysis with user-centered creativity.

At the core of my design philosophy is the belief that great products emerge from the dialogue between technical possibility and human need. I approach design as an iterative process — testing, refining, and adapting ideas to strike a balance between performance, usability, and aesthetics. Every concept I develop is guided by the principle that technology should not only work well, but also feel intuitive and engaging in the hands of the user. By combining material science insight with empathetic design thinking, I aim to create solutions that feel natural, enhance everyday life, and open the door to new ways of experiencing the world.
